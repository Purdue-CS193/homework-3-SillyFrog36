Q1: What are the 3 commands to transfer something from your local codebase to GitHub?
git push, git commit, and git add.

Q2: What does 'git clone' do? 
It creates a clone of a repository.

Q3: What does 'git status' do?
It compares the status of your local branch to the master branch to compare differences and determine of it is up to date.

Q4: What does "Your branch is up to date with 'origin/master'" tell you?
Your local branch is up to date with the most recently fetched version of the master branch.
